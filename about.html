ABOUT

I’m passionate about solving tough customer problems by combining thoughtful research, clever technology and beautiful design.

My appreciation for beauty and design began while growing up in Italy, where I was lucky to be surrounded by a rich culture and timeless art.

I earned a bachelors degree in Aerospace Engineering from University of Padua (Italy), which fostered my ability to solve complex problems by breaking them down.

After graduation I spent time volunteering in India, which taught me how to be resilient when facing unforeseen challenges, and embrace diversity.

My core values are transparency, kindness, integrity and patience.

GOALS

  • To create life-changing, sustainable, inclusive and enjoyable products.
  • To spread awareness around healthy and/or sustainable products
  • To design products that improve the world.

PAIN POINTS
