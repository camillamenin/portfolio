ABOUT THE PROJECT

My goal for this project was to design something new every day for 100 days with the purpose of improving my visual skills.


The biggest challenge was to be consistent in designing something fresh every day. The time crunch forced me to accept a “done is better than perfect” mentality and rely on my instincts.
